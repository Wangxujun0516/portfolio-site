Writing Sample

Localization and UI Copy Case Study

This sample demonstrates how I review English UI text for clarity, consistency, and usability for international users.

Scenario

A product team is preparing an admin console for global release. Several interface strings are understandable, but awkward or unclear for English-speaking users.

Review Principles

  • Prefer clear action verbs.
  • Reduce ambiguity and grammar issues.
  • Keep labels concise and consistent.
  • Match the tone to enterprise software expectations.

Before and After Examples

Original Improved Why
Click here to do configuration Configure settings Shorter, clearer, and more natural in UI space.
Data refresh success Data refreshed successfully Uses standard status-message phrasing.
Please input your mail address Enter your email address More natural verb choice and standard terminology.
The task execute failed Task execution failed Improves grammar and aligns with technical product tone.

Outcome

This kind of review improves product professionalism, reduces user friction, and supports trust for international customers. It also helps unify terminology across the product interface and documentation set.
